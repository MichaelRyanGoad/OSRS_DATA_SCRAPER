==Other==
*[[Observatory Quest]] - 875 experience in [[Attack]], [[Strength]], [[Defence]], or [[Hitpoints]] is a possible reward from completing the quest, depending on the constellation players observe, which is random. Some constellations do not give experience in any skill but give items instead.
*The [[Varrock Museum]] - After completing a specific set of quests, players can speak to [[Historian Minas]] to inform him of their endeavours and their discoveries during these quests. Players will receive [[Kudos]] in addition to lamps which grant experience in a skill of the player's choosing.

==Trivia==
*The [[Waterfall Quest]] grants the most [[experience]] for any [[quest]] with no requirements, which allows a player to level up both their [[Attack]] and [[Strength]] from level 1 to level 30.
